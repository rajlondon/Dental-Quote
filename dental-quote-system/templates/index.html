Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about dental tourism

Yes, dental treatment in Turkey is very safe. The clinics we partner with are modern, equipped with state-of-the-art technology, and staffed by highly qualified dentists, many of whom have trained internationally. All clinics adhere to strict sterilization and hygiene protocols that meet or exceed international standards.

Patients typically save between 50-70% on dental treatments in Turkey compared to prices in the UK, US, and other Western countries. For example, a dental implant that might cost £2,000-£3,000 in the UK could cost as little as £500-£700 in Turkey. The significant savings often cover the cost of flights and accommodation, making it an economical choice even with travel expenses.

The length of stay depends on the treatment. Simple procedures like teeth whitening or basic fillings can be completed in 1-2 days. More complex treatments like implants typically require 5-7 days for the initial visit and another 3-5 days for a follow-up visit after several months. Veneers and crowns usually require 5-7 days to complete. Our dental tourism consultants will provide you with a detailed timeline based on your specific treatment plan.

All clinics we work with provide guarantees on their work, typically ranging from 2-5 years depending on the treatment. If you experience any complications after returning home, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. We can arrange video consultations with your dentist in Turkey and, if necessary, coordinate with local dentists in your area. In rare cases where you need to return to Turkey for adjustments, most clinics will provide the treatment free of charge (though travel expenses would be your responsibility).

Yes, all the dentists at our partner clinics speak fluent English. Additionally, each clinic has international patient coordinators who are multilingual and can assist with any communication needs. This ensures you can discuss your treatment in detail and have all your questions answered clearly.
