Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about dental tourism

Dental tourism allows you to receive high-quality dental treatment in another country at a significantly lower cost. With MyDentalFly, the process is simple:

  1. Get a personalized quote by selecting your treatments
  2. Have a free online consultation with a dental specialist
  3. Book your treatment dates and travel arrangements
  4. Travel to your chosen destination for treatment
  5. Return home with your new smile, often saving 50-70% compared to local prices

We handle all the arrangements, from airport transfers to accommodation, so you can focus on your treatment.

Absolutely. Our partner clinics in Turkey and other destinations offer world-class dental care that often exceeds the standards in Western countries. Here's why:

  • All our partner clinics are carefully vetted and must meet strict quality standards
  • The dentists are highly trained, with many educated in the US, UK, or EU
  • The clinics use the same or better technology and materials as those used in Western countries
  • Turkey and other dental tourism destinations specialize in cosmetic and restorative dentistry, performing far more procedures annually than typical Western dentists
  • All treatments come with a 5-year guarantee for your peace of mind

The savings are substantial, typically between 50-70% compared to UK, US, and EU prices, even after including travel and accommodation costs. For example:

  • A single dental implant that costs £2,000 in the UK might cost just £650 in Turkey
  • A full smile makeover that costs £25,000 in the UK can be done for around £8,500
  • Even with travel and accommodation expenses, patients typically save thousands of pounds on extensive dental work

For patients needing multiple treatments, the savings can be life-changing, making procedures affordable that would otherwise be out of reach.

The length of stay depends on your specific treatment plan:

  • Simple procedures (fillings, teeth whitening, simple crowns): 2-3 days
  • Veneers or multiple crowns: 5-7 days
  • Dental implants: Usually requires 2 visits - first visit of 3-5 days for implant placement, then a second visit of 3-5 days after 3-6 months for the crown placement
  • Full mouth reconstruction: 7-14 days, potentially with a follow-up visit

During your consultation, your dental specialist will provide an exact timeline based on your specific treatment needs.

We've got you covered for follow-up care:

  • All treatments come with a 5-year guarantee
  • Our partner clinics provide detailed aftercare instructions
  • You'll have access to 24/7 online support for any questions or concerns
  • If you need to see a dentist at home, we can provide all your treatment records
  • For any issues covered by our guarantee, we'll arrange and cover the cost of return treatment

Your peace of mind is our priority, and we're committed to ensuring your long-term satisfaction with your dental treatment.
